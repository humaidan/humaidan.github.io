My ORAT Journey [ICT]


My ORAT journey at Bahrain International Airport's new terminal was a testament to the power of collaboration and preparation.

The comprehensive testing program helped identify any issues or inefficiencies in the airport's processes and systems, which were then addressed before the terminal's official opening.

The new terminal has since become a shining example of modern airport design and functionality, and is sure to provide a world-class travel experience for years to come.

2016

Initial release of tender for the full ICT Package of the New Terminal for a full state of the art systems that can fully operate the New Terminal.

Main Contractor was a joint venture between Arabtec and TAV under the management of Hill Internationals & design of ADPi in which Thales/SITA joint venture won the ICT Package sub-tender.

2017

Working extensively with the contractors to review and assist in the High Level Design, Low Level Design, Business Use Cases Documents, Detailed Interface Documents.

2018

Fraport ORAT team joined for Operational testing of new facilities and tracking/reporting progress of ICT Package with contractors, airlines, management, and our engineers.

2019

BAC team is taking the lead in ORAT process.
The new passenger terminal at Bahrain International Airport has successfully completed all preliminary and advanced Operational readiness trials and move to Transition phase.

2020

Soft Opening of New Terminal which marks one of the seamless transfers in history of ORATs from old Terminal to new Terminal.
Spanning 210,000 sqm, the new terminal is four times larger than the existing airport and will increase BIAâ€™s capacity to 14 million passengers a year. It is the largest green building in Bahrain.

Highlights of the new terminal include 8 baggage reclaim belts, 104 check-in counters, 2 halls for registration of first and business class passengers, 36 passport control offices, 12 e-gates for Departures, 8 e-gates for Arrivals, 8,520 sqm of the Check-in area, a 4,780 sqm Departures hall, a 6,600 sqm Arrivals hall, 10,002 sqm of retail space, and 3,300 sqm of Duty-Free space for Departures, and 541 sqm for Arrivals.

The Duty-Free Area at the new terminal is three times larger than that of the current airport, offering 30 leading brands in Bahrain for the first time.

2021

In March 2021, the New Terminal was officially opened by King Hamad bin Isa Al Khalifa
